Welcome to JAMB TRAINING PLATFORM

Mock JAMB Training Platform for Secondary School Students," is an online platform that helps Nigerian secondary school students prepare for the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) exam.

Register  Login

Objectives:

  • Enhance students' familiarity with JAMB exam format and content
  • Improve students' performance through practice and feedback
  • Reduce exam anxiety and stress
  • Provide teachers with valuable insights into students' strengths and weaknesses
  • Increase accessibility and convenience for students nationwide

Project Overview

The platform will provide a simulated JAMB examination experience, offering:

  • Interactive test materials (quizzes)
  • Timed mock exams and assessments
  • Personalized performance analysis and recommendations
